About FareShare

Split costs, not friendships.

FareShare makes it simple to track and split costs between friends, family, or teammates — no spreadsheets, no stress.

Whether you’re planning a weekend getaway, organizing a group dinner, or managing ongoing shared expenses, FareShare keeps everything clear and fair. Each trip (called a Share) brings everyone together to record and review expenses (Fares), track who paid for what, and instantly see who owes or is owed.

Our Mission

We built FareShare to make group spending transparent and effortless. We know how quickly shared costs can become confusing — especially during travel or events. With FareShare, you can focus on the moments that matter, while we handle the math in the background.

Key Features

  • Create a Share for any trip or activity
  • Add participants and log shared Fares
  • Automatically calculate balances for everyone
  • View summaries by category or participant
  • Keep your group finances fair and simple

Why “FareShare”?

Because everyone deserves a fair share — and it just so happens to rhyme.
